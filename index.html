About Heameda

Hope, care and cure

Founded in 2010 in Upanga and relocated in 2017 to our current home in Bunju B, Heameda Hospital has grown into a trusted healthcare provider for Dar es Salaam — committed to compassionate, specialised care for every patient who walks through our doors.

  • ✓ 24/7 emergency & critical care
  • ✓ Cardiac catheterization & pacemaker services
  • ✓ On-site dialysis, laboratory & imaging
